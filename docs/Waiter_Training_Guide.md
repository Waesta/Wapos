# Waiter Training Guide
## WAPOS - Service Staff Operations

---

# Your Role

As a waiter, you are responsible for:
- Taking customer orders
- Entering orders into the system
- Delivering food and drinks
- Processing payments
- Providing excellent customer service

---

# Getting Started

## Logging In

1. Go to the WAPOS login page
2. Enter your **username** and **password**
3. Click **Login**
4. You'll see the Bar POS or Restaurant POS based on your assignment

## Your Dashboard

After login, you'll see:
- **Open Tabs**: Your current active orders
- **Products**: Menu items to add
- **Quick Actions**: Common tasks

---

# Taking Orders

## Step 1: Open a Tab

1. Click **[+ New Tab]** button
2. Enter the **guest's name** (or table number)
3. Select your **station**
4. Click **[Open Tab]**

*The tab now appears in your list on the left*

## Step 2: Add Items

1. **Click on the tab** to select it
2. **Find the item** the customer wants:
   - Browse by category (Beers, Spirits, Wine, etc.)
   - Or type in the search box
3. **Click the item** to add it

### For Drinks with Portions (Spirits, Wine):

When you click a spirit or wine, a popup appears:

1. Select the **portion size**:
   - **Tot**: 25ml, 35ml, or 50ml
   - **Shot**: 30ml, 44ml, or 60ml
   - **Glass**: 125ml, 175ml, or 250ml
2. Enter **quantity** (how many of that portion)
3. Click **[Add to Tab]**

### For Regular Items (Beer, Soft Drinks, Food):

1. Click the item
2. Enter quantity if more than 1
3. Click **[Add]**

## Step 3: Confirm the Order

- Review items in the order panel (right side)
- Verify quantities and prices
- Make any corrections before leaving the table

---

# Customer Asks for the Bill

## Printing the Bill

1. Select the customer's tab
2. Click the **⋮** menu (three dots)
3. Click **"Print Bill (Customer)"**
4. Take the printed bill to the customer

## What's on the Bill

- Business name
- Tab number
- All items ordered with prices
- Subtotal, tax, discounts
- **TOTAL DUE** (highlighted)
- Your name as the server

## After Customer Reviews

- Customer tells you how they want to pay
- Go back to POS to process payment
- Or hand off to cashier

---

# Processing Payments

## If You Process Payment

1. Select the tab
2. Click **[Pay]**
3. Select payment method:

| Method | What to Do |
|--------|------------|
| **Cash** | Enter amount received, give change |
| **Card** | Process on card machine |
| **M-Pesa** | Enter customer's phone number |

4. Click **[Complete Payment]**
5. Print receipt when prompted
6. Give receipt to customer

## If Cashier Processes Payment

1. Tell cashier the tab name/number
2. Cashier processes payment
3. Collect receipt from cashier
4. Deliver to customer

---

# Tips

## Adding Tips to a Tab

1. Select the tab
2. Click **[Add Tip]**
3. Enter the tip amount
4. Click **[Save]**

## Tips at Payment

- Tips can also be added during payment
- Enter tip amount in the tip field
- Tip is recorded with your name

---

# Transferring a Tab

*When you need to hand off to another waiter:*

1. Select the tab
2. Click **⋮** → **"Transfer Tab"**
3. Select the waiter taking over
4. Enter reason (e.g., "End of shift")
5. Click **[Transfer]**

The tab now belongs to the other waiter.

---

# Special Situations

## Customer Wants to Change Order

**Remove an item:**
1. Click the item in the order
2. Click **[Void Item]**
3. Enter reason
4. Confirm

**Add more items:**
- Simply add new items as normal

## Customer Complaint

1. Call your manager
2. Manager may apply discount
3. Document the issue

## Customer Leaves Without Paying

1. **Do NOT void the tab yourself**
2. Inform your manager immediately
3. Manager will handle the situation

## Split Bill

*When customers want to pay separately:*

1. Click **[Pay]** → **"Split Payment"**
2. Enter first person's amount
3. Process their payment
4. Enter next person's amount
5. Continue until total is covered

---

# End of Your Shift

## Before You Leave

1. **Check your open tabs** - all should be closed or transferred
2. **Transfer any remaining tabs** to the next waiter
3. **Check your tips** - Reports → My Performance
4. **Log out** - never leave logged in

## Handover Checklist

- [ ] All my tabs closed or transferred
- [ ] Informed next waiter of any special situations
- [ ] Checked my sales/tips for the shift
- [ ] Logged out of system

---

# Quick Tips for Success

## Do's ✅

- Always confirm orders before leaving table
- Keep the system updated in real-time
- Print bills promptly when requested
- Be accurate with portions and quantities
- Log out when leaving your station

## Don'ts ❌

- Don't share your login with others
- Don't void items without manager approval
- Don't leave tabs open unnecessarily
- Don't process payments for other waiters' tabs without transfer
- Don't ignore system errors - report them

# Common Questions

**Q: I made a mistake adding an item. What do I do?**
A: Void the item immediately and add the correct one. Enter "wrong item" as the reason.

**Q: Customer says the price is wrong.**
A: Check the menu, verify with manager. If error, manager can adjust.

**Q: System is slow or not responding.**
A: Refresh the page (F5). If still slow, inform manager.

**Q: I can't find a product.**
A: Use search, check all categories. If not found, ask manager to add it.

**Q: Customer wants to pay later.**
A: Keep tab open. Get manager approval for large amounts.
